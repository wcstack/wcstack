wcstack: speech echo

The two halves of @wcstack/speech in one loop. <wcs-listen> recognizes your voice into state (event-token); <wcs-speak> reads it back on demand (command-token). The echo-loop guard lives in state — echoIt() refuses to speak while listening — so the synthesized audio is never re-recognized.
Chrome required (SpeechRecognition is vendor-prefixed). Allow the microphone when prompted.
